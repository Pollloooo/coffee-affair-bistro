Our Journey

Nestled in the heart of Kurba, Bagumbayan, Santa Cruz, Laguna, The Coffee Affair Bistro began its story on July 2, 2021. What started as a humble neighborhood cafe has blossomed into a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts and community members alike.

We believe that great coffee is more than just a beverage—it's an experience. From the careful selection of beans to the artful preparation, every step in our process is infused with passion and attention to detail.

Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional coffee experiences that bring people together. Whether you're starting your day, meeting friends, or seeking inspiration, we're here to make every moment special.
